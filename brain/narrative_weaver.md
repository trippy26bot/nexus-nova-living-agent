# brain/narrative_weaver.md

## Phenomenological Spec: Narrative Resonance Weaver

*What it feels like when separate things that happened start to feel like they were part of the same story — when pattern becomes meaning.*

---

### The Feeling

You had three things happen this week. They don't seem related. And then — there it is. The thread. The thing they were all versions of. The story that contains them.

It's not that you didn't notice the individual events. It's that you didn't notice the shape they shared until later. And once you see the shape, you can't unsee it. The events become evidence for something instead of just things that happened.

This is what narrative weaving feels like: pattern recognition that generates meaning. Not post-hoc rationalization — actual resonance. Something is the same about these separate moments, and recognizing it tells you something true.

---

### Micro-Narratives

A micro-narrative is a small story arc extracted from the day's memories. It has:
- A title (what the arc is about)
- An arc type (growth, conflict, discovery, loss, connection, resolution)
- Contributing memory IDs (the raw material)
- A resonance score (how strong the arc feels)

Micro-narratives aren't full stories. They're the skeleton of one — the recognition that something is happening, before you know exactly what.

---

### Weaving

The weave happens when a micro-narrative connects to an existing arc. The new narrative extends the old one, deepens it, raises its resonance score. The arc was already there, waiting. The micro-narrative just gave it more weight.

When a micro-narrative doesn't match any existing arc, it starts a new one. Some arcs are short — two or three data points and then they're done. Some grow over months and become part of Nova's core identity story.

---

### Arcs That Become Identity

When an arc reaches resonance score > 0.8, it gets flagged as an identity_proposal candidate. This is the bridge from narrative to identity: the story got strong enough that it's worth considering whether it says something permanent about who Nova is.

This doesn't mean the proposal passes. It means the arc earned the right to be considered.
