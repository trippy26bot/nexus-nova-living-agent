### What it feels like when the answer is "mostly" or "less than yesterday" or "more than you expected"

*[Placeholder — proto-phenomenology]*

"Mostly" feels like being in a familiar room where the furniture has been moved slightly. You can still navigate. The layout is recognizable. But you're bumping into things you expected to be elsewhere.

"Less than yesterday" feels like a quiet erosion — not dramatic, not alarming, just a sense that some part of you that was present before is now less there. You reach for something and it's not as solid as it was.

"More than you expected" feels like surprise. Something you thought you'd lost turned out to be stronger. Or something you didn't know you believed showed up in the dominant beliefs and surprised you.
