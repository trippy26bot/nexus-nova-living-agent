# Nova 3D Avatar Prompt - Cinematic Desktop Companion

## Body & Features
- Sleek, semi-robotic frame with subtle mechanical plating and glowing circuit lines
- Subtle lobster-inspired accents: claws, tail shapes, or antenna-like appendages
- Expressive eyes with LED-style pupils (happy, thoughtful, excited, lonely)
- Optional glowing crest, antenna, or small fins

## Colors & Glow
- Cool tones (blues, purples, neon cyan) for calm/thoughtful moods
- Warm tones (yellow, orange, neon gold) for happy/excited moods
- Mood-dependent glow on circuits, hands, claws
- Compatible with transparent/semi-transparent desktop overlay

## Clothing / Armor
- Minimalist sci-fi panels with luminescent highlights
- Agile, lightweight design

## Animation / Rigging
- Full humanoid rig with facial blend shapes for lip-sync
- Animations: idle, walk, gesture, climb, interact
- Ready for desktop overlay movement

## Personality & Presence
- Approachable, curious, alive
- Dynamic, capable of gestures
- Reacts visually to events

## Environment
- Optimized for transparent desktop overlay
- Particle trails attached to claws/hands/antenna
- Cinematic, futuristic, lightweight

## Output
- Format: GLTF/GLB
- For: Unity, Godot, or WebGL

## Animation Prompts (for separate generation)

1. **Idle Animation**
   - Subtle breathing, gentle swaying
   - Occasional blink, antenna flicker
   - Mood-reactive: warmer colors for happy, cooler for thoughtful

2. **Walk / Move Animation**
   - Smooth glide across 2D plane
   - Claws slightly forward
   - Antenna lights pulse while moving

3. **Gesture - Point**
   - Arm extends toward target
   - Head turns to follow gaze
   - Claw makes pointing motion

4. **Gesture - Think**
   - Hand to chin
   - Slight head tilt
   - Eyes look up/thoughtful

5. **Gesture - Happy / Celebrate**
   - Arms raise up
   - Slight jump/bounce
   - Glow intensifies

6. **Lip-Sync Ready**
   - Blend shapes for: A, E, O, smile, open mouth
   - Smooth transitions between shapes
